Vote on 2026 Budget

The 2026 budget meeting was held on Wednesday, December 18 2024 at 6:08 pm via Zoom. The transcript can be viewed here: Transcript

The vote was held from December 10 until December 26. Voting was conducted online via OpaVote. Jonathan Cervas served as the election administrator.

Results

Only one question was on ballot - Do you approve the budget?

Voter Turnout

A total of 95 out of 190 units voted (50%).

A total of 516,451 out of 999,989 votes were cast (51.6%)
