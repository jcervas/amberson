# Amberson Towers May 2026 Bylaws Vote

The *Amberson Towers Condominium Association Council* held the annual owners meeting on Monday, May 11, 2026 at 7:00pm. At that meeting, a discussion about changing the bylaws occurred. 

**Currently~**
ARTICLE IV: Meetings and Voting Rights of Unit Owner
Section 2 (b) Budget Meeting. A meeting of Unit Owners to approve the annual budget shall be held in the month of December on a date and time as shall be decided by the Council.

**Proposed Change~**
ARTICLE IV: Meetings and Voting Rights of Unit Owner
Section 2 (b) Budget Meeting. A meeting of Unit Owners to approve the annual budget shall be held in the month of November on a date and time as shall be decided by the Council.

*Amending bylaws requires greater participation than a budget or council vote. Voting will continue until we have reached quorum.*

<In addition to the bylaw vote, we are simultaneously holding an election for Council. That vote is on a separate ballot.>
